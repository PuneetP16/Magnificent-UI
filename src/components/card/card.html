Card

The Card component has 6 variants ,
1. Vertical Cards,
2. Horizontal Cards,
3. Cards with badges,
4. Cards with dismiss,
5. Cards with text overlay and
6. Text only cards.

Use following classes with class "card" to get that style of the card. card card--vertical card--horizontal card--badge card--dismiss card--text_overlay card--only_text
